<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photocuring agents are essential in polymerization processes, primarily categorized into free-radical and cationic types. Free-radical photocuring agents generate reactive radicals upon exposure to UV or visible light, initiating polymerization in materials like coatings and adhesives. Conversely, cationic photocuring agents create cationic species, facilitating the polymerization of epoxy and vinyl ethers, often resulting in more stable and chemically resistant products. Both types serve diverse applications in industries such as printing, electronics, and coatings, each offering unique advantages.</p></p>
