<p><p>The Lipids Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Simple Lipids** are composed of fatty acids and glycerol, primarily serving as energy sources or storage in formulations. </p><p>2. **Compound Lipids** consist of modified simple lipids, incorporating other groups like phosphates or sugars, enhancing their functional properties in pharmaceuticals. </p><p>3. **Derived Lipids** are products of the breakdown of simple and compound lipids, offering specialized functions and enhancing drug bioavailability. Each type plays a crucial role in drug formulation and delivery.</p></p>
