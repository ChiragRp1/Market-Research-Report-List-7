<p><p>The pop filter market predominantly features two main types: nylon and metal. Nylon pop filters utilize a thin, flexible mesh fabric that effectively reduces plosive sounds without significantly altering tone. They are lightweight and portable, making them popular among home studio users. Metal pop filters, on the other hand, consist of a sturdy metal screen, providing durability and ease of cleaning. They offer a more consistent sound reduction and are favored in professional studios for their longevity and sound clarity.</p></p>
