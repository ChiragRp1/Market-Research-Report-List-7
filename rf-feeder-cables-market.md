<p><p>The RF feeder cables market includes coaxial cables and open wire or twin feeder cables. Coaxial cables are widely used for their ability to transmit high-frequency signals with minimal loss, featuring a central conductor, insulating layer, and outer shield. Open wire or twin feeders consist of two parallel conductors and are preferable for certain broadcasting applications due to their low loss characteristics at specific frequencies. Both types cater to different needs in telecommunications, broadcasting, and wireless communication systems.</p></p>
