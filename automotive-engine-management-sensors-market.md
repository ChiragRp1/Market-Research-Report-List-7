<p><p>The automotive engine management sensors market encompasses various types, each playing a crucial role in optimizing engine performance. Mass airflow sensors measure the amount of air entering the engine for accurate fuel mixture adjustments, while knock sensors detect engine knock and help prevent damage. Crankshaft sensors monitor the crankshaft position for timing, whereas camshaft sensors do the same for camshaft position. Pressure sensors gauge various pressurized systems, and temperature sensors assess engine temperature, ensuring efficient functioning and emissions control.</p></p>
