<p><p>The dip welded wire mesh market consists of two primary types: electro galvanized welded wire mesh and hot dipped galvanized steel mesh. Electro galvanized welded wire mesh involves a process where steel wires are coated with a thin layer of zinc for corrosion resistance, making it suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, hot dipped galvanized steel mesh undergoes a more robust coating process, providing enhanced protection against severe weather and abrasion, making it ideal for outdoor use and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
