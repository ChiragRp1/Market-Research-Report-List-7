<p><p>The multifunction monitoring relays market can be segmented into 1-phase and 3-phase categories. 1-phase relays are designed for single-phase electrical systems, providing essential monitoring for overloads, voltage fluctuations, and phase imbalance, making them ideal for residential and small commercial applications. In contrast, 3-phase relays cater to more complex industrial environments, offering comprehensive monitoring of three-phase systems to ensure balanced loads, detect faults, and enhance energy efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing electrical system performance and preventing failures.</p></p>
