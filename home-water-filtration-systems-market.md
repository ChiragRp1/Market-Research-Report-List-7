<p><p>Home water filtration systems are categorized into several types based on their filtration methods. Reverse Osmosis (RO) filtration uses a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants and impurities from water, providing highly purified water. UV filtration employs ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. Carbon filtration uses activated carbon to absorb odors, chlorine, and some chemicals, enhancing taste and quality. Other market types may include multi-stage systems or ceramic filters, offering diverse options to meet various water quality needs.</p></p>
