<p><strong>The Passenger Car Brake Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger car brake fluids are categorized primarily into three types: DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 is a glycol-based fluid, widely used for its affordability and adequate performance in most conditions. DOT 4, also glycol-based but with higher boiling points, provides improved performance for vehicles with anti-lock braking systems. DOT 5, in contrast, is silicone-based, offering superior moisture resistance but is not compatible with all systems. These distinctions influence performance, safety, and vehicle compatibility in various driving conditions.</p></p>
