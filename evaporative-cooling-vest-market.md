<p><p>Evaporative cooling vests are specialized garments designed to lower body temperature through the process of evaporation. These vests typically contain water-retaining materials that absorb water and release it gradually, allowing the wearer to experience a cooling effect as the moisture evaporates. They are widely used in various settings, including outdoor sports, industrial applications, and healthcare, where heat stress is a concern.</p><p>The Evaporative Cooling Vest Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to rising awareness of heat-related illnesses, a growing focus on employee safety in industrial environments, and increasing participation in outdoor activities.
