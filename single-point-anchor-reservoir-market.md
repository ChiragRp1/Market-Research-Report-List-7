<p><p>The Single Point Anchor Reservoir (SPAR) market encompasses three key types: Classic Spar, Truss Spar, and Cell Spar. The Classic Spar features a vertical cylindrical shape and offers stability and efficiency in deep-water locations. The Truss Spar uses a truss-like structure for enhanced strength and weight distribution, making it suitable for harsher environments. The Cell Spar employs large, buoyant cylindrical cells to provide additional storage and stability, making it ideal for deep-sea oil production and exploration. Together, these designs cater to various offshore needs.</p></p>
