<p><strong>The Indirect Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Coiled Type</li><li>External Submerged Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indirect water heaters utilize a secondary heat source to warm water, typically engaging a boiler for heating. The Internal Coiled Type features a coil submerged within the water tank, allowing efficient heat transfer as water circulates through the coil. On the other hand, the External Submerged Type has a heating element placed outside the tank, with water heated while in contact with the external device. Both types serve to enhance energy efficiency in heating systems by utilizing existing boilers effectively.</p></p>
