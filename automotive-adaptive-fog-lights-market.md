<p><p>Automotive adaptive fog lights enhance visibility in low-visibility conditions by adjusting beam patterns based on driving scenarios. **High-Intensity Discharge (HID) Lights** offer bright, energy-efficient illumination with a longer lifespan. **LED Lights** are highly durable, energy-efficient, and provide instant brightness with a compact design. **Halogen Lights** are cost-effective, widely available, and produce warm light but are less efficient and have a shorter lifespan compared to HID and LED. Each type caters to different performance, efficiency, and budget needs in the automotive fog light market.</p></p>
