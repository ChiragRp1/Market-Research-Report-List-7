<p><ul><li>Optical Turnstile</li><li>Arm Turnstile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transportation turnstile market is divided into two main types: optical turnstiles and arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles utilize advanced sensors to detect and authorize entry, allowing for a seamless and efficient flow of passengers while maintaining security. They are often favored in high-traffic areas for their modern design and minimal physical barriers. In contrast, arm turnstiles feature rotating arms that physically block passage until access is granted, providing a robust security solution, particularly in environments requiring higher control levels.</p></p>
