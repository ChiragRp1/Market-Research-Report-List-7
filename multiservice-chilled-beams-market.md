<p><p>The multiservice chilled beams market primarily includes two types: bare type and mosaic. Bare type chilled beams offer a simple design with minimal features, primarily focused on cooling and heating through the circulation of water. In contrast, mosaic chilled beams integrate additional services, such as lighting and ventilation, into a cohesive unit, enhancing aesthetics and energy efficiency. Both types cater to varying user needs, with bare type suited for cost-effective solutions and mosaic type appealing for more sophisticated architectural designs.</p></p>
