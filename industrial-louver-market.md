<p><ul><li>Fixed Blade</li><li>Adjustable Blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial louver market consists of two primary types: fixed blade and adjustable blade louvers. Fixed blade louvers are designed with stationary blades that provide consistent airflow while blocking rain and debris, making them suitable for ventilation in various industrial settings. In contrast, adjustable blade louvers allow users to change the blade angle to control airflow and exposure to external elements, providing greater versatility for dynamic environmental conditions. Both types are essential for optimizing ventilation and maintaining air quality in industrial applications.</p></p>
