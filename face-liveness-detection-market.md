<p><p>The Face Liveness Detection market is divided into two main types: Active and Passive Facial Liveness Detection. Active Facial Liveness Detection requires user interaction, such as blinking or turning the head, to verify that a real person is present during facial recognition. In contrast, Passive Facial Liveness Detection operates without user involvement, analyzing attributes like reflections or movement to distinguish between live faces and photos or masks. Both methods enhance security and authenticity in biometric systems.</p></p>
