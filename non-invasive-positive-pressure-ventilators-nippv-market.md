<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NiPPV) are medical devices that assist patients with respiratory challenges without the need for invasive procedures. The market mainly comprises two types: CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) machines, which deliver a consistent airflow to keep airways open, and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) machines, which provide varying pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation. Both devices are crucial for managing conditions such as sleep apnea and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), improving patient comfort and oxygenation.</p></p>
