<p><p>Residential Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) are essential electrical devices that protect circuits from overloads and short circuits. They come in various types based on the number of poles: 1-pole MCBs protect single-phase circuits, suitable for lighting and small appliances. 2-pole MCBs handle single-phase circuits with a neutral connection, while 3-pole MCBs are designed for three-phase circuits, commonly used in larger appliances. 4-pole MCBs serve three-phase systems with a neutral, providing comprehensive protection for complex residential setups.</p></p>
