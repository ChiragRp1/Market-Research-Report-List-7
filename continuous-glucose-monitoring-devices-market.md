<p><p>The Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices market encompasses several key segments: sensors, transmitters and receivers, and insulin pumps. Sensors continuously measure glucose levels in the interstitial fluid, providing real-time data. Transmitters relay this information wirelessly to receivers or compatible devices for user accessibility. Insulin pumps, often integrated with CGM systems, deliver precise doses of insulin based on glucose readings, enhancing diabetes management. Together, these components facilitate improved blood sugar control and empower patients in their daily health management.</p></p>
