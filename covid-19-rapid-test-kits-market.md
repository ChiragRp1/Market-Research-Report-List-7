<p><p>The COVID-19 rapid test kits market consists of several types. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide immediate results using nasal swabs or blood samples, facilitating quick screening. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) tests detect antibodies or antigens, offering higher sensitivity and quantification but requiring laboratory processing. Neutralization Assays assess the presence of antibodies capable of neutralizing the virus, crucial for evaluating vaccine efficacy and immunity. Each type plays a vital role in diagnosing and managing COVID-19 spread and response.</p></p>
