<p><p>Flangeless butterfly valves are essential in fluid control systems, categorized mainly into two types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves utilize elastomeric materials for sealing, providing excellent leakage prevention and suitability for low to medium pressure applications. In contrast, hard seal valves feature metal-to-metal contact, offering superior durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, making them ideal for demanding industrial environments. Both types cater to diverse applications in various sectors, including water treatment, oil, and gas industries.</p></p>
