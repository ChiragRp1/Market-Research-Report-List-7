<p><strong>The Camera Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Master Chip</li><li>Photosensitive Chip</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera chips market is segmented into three primary types: Master Chips, Photosensitive Chips, and Others. Master Chips act as the brain of the camera, processing image data and controlling various functions. Photosensitive Chips, typically CCD or CMOS sensors, capture light and convert it into electrical signals for image formation. The "Others" category includes additional components like signal processors, lens drivers, and auxiliary chips that enhance functionality. Each type plays a vital role in the overall performance and quality of imaging systems.</p></p>
