<p><p>Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CMMS is hosted on remote servers, offering accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection, along with lower upfront costs and easier updates. On-premises CMMS, on the other hand, is installed locally on company servers, providing more control and customization but often requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options aim to streamline maintenance processes and improve asset management efficiency.</p></p>
