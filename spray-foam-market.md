<p><p>Spray foam is a versatile insulation material made by mixing and reacting two chemical components, isocyanate and polyol resin, which expand upon application to form a rigid or flexible foam. Spray foam is available in two primary types: open-cell and closed-cell, each offering distinct properties for different applications.</p><p>The Spray Foam Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient building solutions and sustainable construction practices. Geographically, North America and Europe dominate the market, while Asia-Pacific is emerging as a high-growth region due to rapid urbanization and infrastructure development. <p><p>The spray foam market includes light-density and medium-density types. Light-density spray foam, also known as open-cell foam, is lightweight, flexible, and provides excellent insulation with soundproofing properties. It is ideal for interior applications like walls and ceilings. Medium-density spray foam, or closed-cell foam, is denser, rigid, and offers higher insulation value, moisture resistance, and structural strength. It is commonly used in exterior applications, roofs, and areas requiring durability. Both types cater to diverse insulation needs in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
