<p><p>Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) systems encompass various types that enhance vehicle efficiency and performance. Belt-Chain Drive CVTs use a belt and pulley system to provide seamless gear ratios, ideal for smaller vehicles due to their compact design. In contrast, Planetary CVTs utilize a set of gears arranged in a planetary configuration, allowing for smoother power delivery and better torque management, making them suitable for automotive applications. Both types contribute to improved fuel economy and driving comfort in modern vehicles.</p></p>
