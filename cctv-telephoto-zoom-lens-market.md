<p><strong>The CCTV Telephoto Zoom Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cs Mount</li><li>C Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCTV telephoto zoom lens market is primarily categorized into two types: CS mount and C mount lenses. CS mount lenses feature a shorter flange focal distance, making them suitable for compact cameras and allowing for easier installation in tight spaces. In contrast, C mount lenses have a longer flange distance, providing greater versatility and compatibility with a wider range of cameras. Both types are essential for capturing detailed images over long distances, enhancing surveillance capabilities in various environments.</p></p>
