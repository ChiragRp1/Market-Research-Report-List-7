<p><ul><li>Enhancement Processor</li><li>Restoration Processor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Bass Processor market comprises two main types: Enhancement Processors and Restoration Processors. Enhancement Processors focus on augmenting bass sounds in audio, improving overall sound quality and making low frequencies more prominent. They are commonly used in music production and live sound applications. In contrast, Restoration Processors aim to recover lost low-end frequencies from recordings, correcting issues caused by poor audio quality or storage mediums. Together, these processors cater to diverse needs in audio enhancement and fidelity restoration.</p></p>
