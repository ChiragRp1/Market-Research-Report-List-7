<p><p>The Ocean Thermoelectric Power Generation System market is categorized into three main types: closed cycle systems, open cycle systems, and hybrid cycle systems. Closed cycle systems utilize a working fluid that is heated and cooled in a loop, creating energy without direct ocean water contact. Open cycle systems directly use seawater, vaporizing it to drive turbines. Hybrid cycle systems combine features of both, integrating closed and open cycle principles to optimize efficiency and energy output while minimizing environmental impacts.</p></p>
