<p><p>The PEX oxygen barrier pipes market includes various types, each with unique characteristics. PEX-A pipes, known for their flexibility and resistance to cracking, are produced using the Engel method. PEX-B pipes, made through the peroxide method, offer good stability and are cost-effective. PEX-C pipes utilize the electron beam method, providing enhanced resistance to high temperatures. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine aluminum with PEX layers, offering strength and thermal efficiency. Each type serves specific applications in plumbing, heating, and industrial systems.</p></p>
