<p><p>The Central HVAC market includes two primary types: Split System Central HVAC and Packaged Unit Central HVAC. A Split System Central HVAC consists of indoor and outdoor units, allowing for flexibility in installation and maintenance. It efficiently separates heating and cooling components. In contrast, a Packaged Unit Central HVAC combines all components into a single outdoor unit, simplifying installation and reducing space requirements. Both systems are designed to provide efficient climate control in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
