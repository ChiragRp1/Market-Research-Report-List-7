<p><p>The video door system market can be categorized into two primary types: Wi-Fi and Radio Frequency (RF). Wi-Fi video door systems utilize internet connectivity to transmit video and audio, allowing homeowners to monitor their entrance remotely via smartphones or tablets. In contrast, Radio Frequency video door systems operate on dedicated wireless frequencies, providing a direct connection between the door camera and a monitor inside the home. Both types enhance security and convenience, but they differ in connectivity and range capabilities.</p></p>
