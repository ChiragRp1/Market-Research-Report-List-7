<p><p>The high-resolution SWIR camera market is characterized by two main types: SWIR area scan cameras and SWIR line scan cameras. SWIR area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them ideal for applications requiring full-frame imaging of static scenes. In contrast, SWIR line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, excelling in high-speed applications and continuous monitoring, such as in manufacturing and quality control processes. Both types leverage shortwave infrared technology for enhanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>
