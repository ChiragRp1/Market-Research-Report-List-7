<p><p>Truck bias tires are a type of tire construction where the plies are arranged diagonally across the tire, intersecting at an angle. <p><p>The **Truck Bias Tire Market** includes two main types: **Ordinary Bias Tires** and **Bias Belted Tires**. Ordinary Bias Tires feature crisscrossed ply layers, offering durability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for rugged terrains. Bias Belted Tires add a stabilizing belt under the tread, enhancing stability and reducing heat buildup, making them suitable for mixed-use conditions. Both types cater to heavy-duty applications, with Ordinary Bias Tires excelling in off-road performance and Bias Belted Tires balancing durability and on-road comfort for trucks.</p></p>
