<p><p>The embossed TPU film market is segmented into three main types based on breathability: low, medium, and high breathable TPU films. Low breathable TPU films offer minimal airflow, making them suitable for applications requiring moisture retention and protection. Medium breathable TPU films balance breathability and moisture resistance, ideal for versatile uses in clothing and accessories. High breathable TPU films excel in ventilation, suitable for outdoor gear and performance apparel, enabling enhanced comfort during physical activities by allowing sweat to evaporate efficiently.</p></p>
