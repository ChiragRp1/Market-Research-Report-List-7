<p><strong>The Electric Valve Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Stroke Electric Actuators</li><li>Multi-turn Electric Actuators</li><li>Partial Rotary Electric Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric valve actuators are essential components in automated fluid control systems, classified into three main types: </p><p>1. **Straight Stroke Electric Actuators** move valves in a linear motion, commonly used for gate or globe valves.</p><p>2. **Multi-turn Electric Actuators** enable full rotations to operate valves requiring several turns, ideal for flange or knife gate valves.</p><p>3. **Partial Rotary Electric Actuators** provide limited rotation, suitable for quarter-turn valves like ball or butterfly valves, ensuring precise control and efficient operation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
