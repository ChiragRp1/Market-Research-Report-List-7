<p><p>The dementia treatment market is categorized primarily into Donepezil, Memantine, Rivastigmine, and other medications. Donepezil is an acetylcholinesterase inhibitor that enhances cognitive function in Alzheimer's patients. Memantine, an NMDA receptor antagonist, is used to manage moderate to severe Alzheimer's by regulating glutamate activity. Rivastigmine, another cholinesterase inhibitor, is effective for both Alzheimer's and Parkinson's dementia. The "Others" category includes various treatments, such as herbal supplements and off-label drugs, addressing diverse patient needs and stages of dementia.</p></p>
