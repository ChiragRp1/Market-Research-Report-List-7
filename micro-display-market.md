<p><p>The micro display market comprises several types, each with unique technologies and applications. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) offers high contrast and vibrant colors, making it ideal for wearable devices and head-mounted displays. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) provides high resolution and is commonly used in projectors and augmented reality displays. DLP (Digital Light Processing) utilizes micro mirrors for sharp images, often found in projectors. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) delivers cost-effective solutions for a variety of applications, including monitors and televisions.</p></p>
