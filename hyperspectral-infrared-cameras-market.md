<p><p>The hyperspectral infrared cameras market is categorized into four main types based on wavelength ranges: </p><p>1. **Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR)**: Captures light in the visible spectrum and near-infrared, ideal for applications in agriculture and environmental monitoring.</p><p>2. **Short Wave Infrared (SWIR)**: Operates between visible and medium-wave infrared, useful for detecting moisture and chemical compositions.</p><p>3. **Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR)**: Primarily used in military and surveillance applications, focusing on temperature changes.</p><p>4. **Long Wave Infrared (LWIR)**: Detects thermal radiation, widely used for night vision and thermal imaging.</p></p>
