<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure System</li><li>Vacuum System</li><li>Combination System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic conveyor market encompasses three main types: Positive Pressure Systems, Vacuum Systems, and Combination Systems. Positive Pressure Systems utilize air pressure to transport materials through pipelines, making them ideal for short distances and lightweight materials. Vacuum Systems, on the other hand, employ suction to move materials, suitable for handling fragile items over longer distances. Combination Systems integrate both methods, allowing for versatility in various applications, effectively transporting a wide range of materials while optimizing efficiency and reducing contamination.</p></p>
