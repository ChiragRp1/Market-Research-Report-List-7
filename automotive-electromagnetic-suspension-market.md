<p><ul><li>"Active Electromagnetic Suspension"</li><li>"Passive Electromagnetic Suspension"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electromagnetic suspension market comprises two main types: active and passive electromagnetic suspension systems. Active electromagnetic suspension utilizes real-time feedback and control systems to adjust the suspension characteristics dynamically, enhancing ride comfort and handling. In contrast, passive electromagnetic suspension relies on fixed magnetic materials to provide consistent damping without real-time adjustments, offering a simpler and often more cost-effective solution. Both systems aim to improve vehicle stability, ride quality, and safety, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
