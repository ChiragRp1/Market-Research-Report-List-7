<p><ul><li>Smooth Rotor</li><li>Slotted Rotor</li><li>Drilled Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake rotor market includes three main types: smooth rotors, slotted rotors, and drilled rotors. Smooth rotors are standard, offering consistent performance and durability for everyday driving. Slotted rotors feature grooves that improve heat dissipation and reduce brake fade, ideal for high-performance or heavy-duty use. Drilled rotors have holes for better cooling and water dispersion, enhancing wet-weather performance but may be prone to cracking under extreme stress. Each type caters to specific driving needs, balancing performance, durability, and application.</p></p>
