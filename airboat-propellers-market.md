<p><strong>The Airboat Propellers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Reduction Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airboat propellers are categorized by their drive systems: Direct Drive and Reduction Gear. Direct Drive systems connect the propeller directly to the engine, offering simplicity, lower maintenance, and higher speeds but with less torque. Reduction Gear systems use a gearbox to reduce engine RPM, increasing torque and efficiency, ideal for heavy loads or shallow waters. Both types cater to different operational needs, balancing speed, power, and fuel efficiency for airboat performance in diverse environments.</p></p>
