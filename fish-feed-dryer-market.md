<p><strong>The Fish Feed Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sunken Type</li><li>Floating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fish feed dryer market is primarily categorized into two types: sunken type and floating type. Sunken type dryers produce feed that sinks in water, making them ideal for bottom-feeding fish species. These are typically dense and have a slower breakdown rate. Conversely, floating type dryers create feed that remains on the water's surface, attracting species that feed at that level. Both types are essential for maximizing feed efficiency and optimizing fish growth, catering to diverse aquaculture needs.</p></p>
