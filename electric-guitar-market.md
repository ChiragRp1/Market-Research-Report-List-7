<p><p>The electric guitar market can be divided into three main types: solid body, semi-hollow body, and hollow body guitars. Solid body guitars are known for their sustain and versatility, making them popular in rock and metal genres. Semi-hollow body guitars combine elements of both solid and hollow designs, offering a warmer tone and resonance, suitable for jazz and blues. Hollow body guitars, fully constructed from wood, produce rich, vibrant sounds, ideal for jazz and vintage styles, but are more prone to feedback.</p></p>
