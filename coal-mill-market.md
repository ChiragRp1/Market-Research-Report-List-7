<p><p>The coal mill market is segmented into three main types based on speed: low-speed, medium-speed, and high-speed coal mills. Low-speed coal mills, typically used in larger power plants, operate at about 15-20 RPM and are known for their durability. Medium-speed mills, operating at 40-100 RPM, offer a balance of efficiency and control and are commonly used in various industrial applications. High-speed coal mills, running above 100 RPM, are designed for fine grinding, optimizing combustion efficiency in smaller plants.</p></p>
