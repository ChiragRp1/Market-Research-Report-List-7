<p><p>The brakes market encompasses several key components essential for vehicle safety and performance. Brake pads are friction materials that press against rotors to slow down or stop vehicles. Brake shoes serve a similar function in drum brake systems. Brake linings, found in both pads and shoes, enhance stopping power. Brake rotors are metal discs where the pads clamp down to create friction, while brake drums are cylindrical components that house brake shoes for slower vehicles. Each type plays a critical role in effective braking systems.</p></p>
