<p><p>The mobile MRI scanner market is categorized by field strength: low, mid, and high. Low field strength scanners, typically below 1.0 Tesla, offer portability and reduced cost but lower image quality, ideal for specific applications like pediatrics. Mid field strength scanners, ranging from 1.0 to 1.5 Tesla, balance portability and image detail, suitable for general diagnostics. High field strength scanners, above 1.5 Tesla, deliver exceptional image resolution and speed but are less portable, primarily used in specialized medical facilities.</p></p>
