<p><p>Space frames are structural systems made up of interconnected elements that create a lightweight, rigid framework. The market is categorized into single-layer, double-layer, and triple-layer types. Single-layer space frames consist of one plane of structural members, ideal for small structures. Double-layer space frames feature two layers of framing, offering enhanced strength and stability, suitable for larger spans. Triple-layer space frames provide even greater load distribution and architectural flexibility, making them ideal for complex designs and large buildings like airports and auditoriums.</p></p>
