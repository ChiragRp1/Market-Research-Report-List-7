<p><ul><li>PH Buffer Calibration Solutions</li><li>ORP Buffer Calibration Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calibration solutions are essential for ensuring accurate measurements in various analytical processes. The pH buffer calibration solutions are designed to maintain precise pH levels during testing, helping in the evaluation of acidity or alkalinity in samples. Meanwhile, ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential) buffer calibration solutions are used to measure the electronic potential of a solution, which indicates its oxidizing or reducing capacity. Both types serve vital roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety, supporting quality control and compliance.</p></p>
