<p><ul><li>Hot rolling mill rolls</li><li>Cold rolling mill rolls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mill roll market is divided into two primary types: hot rolling mill rolls and cold rolling mill rolls. Hot rolling mill rolls are used for processing metal at elevated temperatures, allowing for easier shaping and reducing thickness. In contrast, cold rolling mill rolls operate at room temperature, providing enhanced surface finish and tighter tolerances. Each type caters to different industrial applications, with hot rolls focusing on bulk material processing, while cold rolls are suited for precision components in diverse sectors.</p></p>
