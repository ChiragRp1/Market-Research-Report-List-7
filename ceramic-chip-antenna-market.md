<p><ul><li>Dielectric Chip Antennas</li><li>LTCC Chip Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic chip antenna market primarily consists of dielectric chip antennas and LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) chip antennas. Dielectric chip antennas utilize high-permittivity ceramic materials to achieve compact designs and enhanced performance in various frequencies, making them suitable for mobile devices and IoT applications. LTCC chip antennas, crafted through co-fired ceramics, enable multilayer construction, which allows for complex circuit integration and improved reliability. Both types cater to the increasing demand for efficient, compact, and lightweight antenna solutions in modern electronics.</p></p>
