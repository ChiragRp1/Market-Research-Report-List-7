The market for EMATs is divided into two main types: Electromagnet Type, which employs an electromagnet to create variable magnetic fields, offering flexibility in applications, and Permanent Magnet Type, which uses fixed magnets to produce steady ultrasonic signals, ideal for specific, consistent tasks. Both types serve critical roles in non-destructive testing and material characterization.</p></p>
