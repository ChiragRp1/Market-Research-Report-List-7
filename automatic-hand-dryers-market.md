<p><p>The automatic hand dryers market is primarily divided into two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-velocity air streams to quickly evaporate moisture, providing efficient and hygienic drying in a matter of seconds. In contrast, hot air dryers emit warm air to gradually evaporate water from hands, typically taking longer to dry effectively. Both types cater to different user preferences and settings, impacting their adoption in public restrooms, commercial spaces, and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
