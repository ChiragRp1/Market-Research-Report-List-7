<p><p>An automotive brake actuator is a critical component in modern braking systems, responsible for converting energy into mechanical motion to apply or release brakes. It plays a vital role in ensuring vehicle safety, particularly in advanced systems like anti-lock braking systems (ABS), electronic stability control (ESC), and regenerative braking in electric vehicles. These actuators can be hydraulic, electric, or pneumatic, depending on the vehicle type and braking system.</p><p>The Automotive Brake Actuator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
