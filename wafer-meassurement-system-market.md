<p><strong>The Wafer Meassurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-beam Meassurement</li><li>Optical Meassurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Measurement System market is primarily categorized into two types: E-beam measurement and optical measurement. E-beam measurement utilizes electron beams to analyze wafer characteristics with high precision, making it ideal for critical dimensions and surface topology assessments in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, optical measurement employs light-based techniques to gauge wafer parameters, offering faster and non-destructive evaluations. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality and performance of semiconductor devices, catering to different needs within the manufacturing process.</p></p>
