<p><p>The dryer cage market can be categorized into two main types: heat-free and heated. Heat-free dryer cages utilize ambient air to dry materials, relying on natural evaporation processes. This method is energy-efficient and ideal for sensitive items. In contrast, heated dryer cages employ artificial heating elements to accelerate drying, offering faster moisture removal. This approach is suitable for materials requiring quicker turnaround times and can handle larger volumes, making it valuable for industries prioritizing efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
