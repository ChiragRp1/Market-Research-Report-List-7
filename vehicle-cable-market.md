<p><p>The vehicle cable market comprises various insulation materials, each with unique properties. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) offers good flexibility and chemical resistance, while Polypropylene (PP) is lightweight and has excellent thermal properties. Cross-linked Polyethylene (XLPE) is known for its high thermal resistance and durability. Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) provides outstanding abrasion resistance and elasticity. Polyphenylene Ether (PPE) combines excellent electrical insulation with thermal stability. Additionally, other materials may include specialized compounds that cater to unique automotive requirements, enhancing performance and safety.</p></p>
