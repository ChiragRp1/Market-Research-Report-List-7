<p><p>The corrugated board market includes several types: Single Face Board, which consists of one layer of fluting attached to one liner, commonly used for wrapping and cushioning; Single Wall Board, featuring a single fluted layer sandwiched between two liners, ideal for lightweight packaging; Double Wall Board, made up of two fluted layers and three liners, providing enhanced strength for heavier products; and Triple Wall Board, consisting of three fluted layers and four liners, offering maximum durability for robust shipping needs.</p></p>
