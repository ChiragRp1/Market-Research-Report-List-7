<p><p>The laser brazing machine market includes various welding types, notably fillet welding and lap welding. Fillet welding involves joining two surfaces at a right angle, creating a triangular joint that enhances strength and is ideal for structural applications. Lap welding, on the other hand, involves overlapping two metal pieces, providing a broad joint surface that improves load distribution. Both methods utilize precise laser technology, offering high speed, minimal distortion, and excellent control over the welding process, suitable for various industrial sectors.</p></p>
