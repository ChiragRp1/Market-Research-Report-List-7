<p><p>The Auto Lensometer market features two main types: Visual and Projection Lensmeters. Visual Type Lensmeters measure optical properties by allowing operators to view the lens directly, offering hands-on interaction and real-time adjustments. In contrast, Projection Type Lensmeters utilize digital technology to project measurements onto a screen, providing automated readings and higher accuracy. Both types serve to enhance efficiency and precision in optical assessment, catering to different user preferences and operational needs in the vision care industry.</p></p>
