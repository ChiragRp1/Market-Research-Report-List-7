<p><p>The energy-efficient windows market primarily features double glazing and triple low-E glazing. Double glazing consists of two glass panes with an insulating space in between, reducing heat transfer and improving energy efficiency. Triple low-E glazing adds a third pane and specialized coatings, further enhancing insulation and minimizing energy loss. Both options significantly decrease heating and cooling costs, providing homeowners with improved comfort and reduced environmental impact. Their growing popularity reflects an increasing demand for sustainable building solutions.</p></p>
