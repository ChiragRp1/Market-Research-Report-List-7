<p><strong>The Iron and Steel Slag Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Splash Method</li><li>Hot Stuffy Method</li><li>Roller Method</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron and steel slag processing involves converting molten slag into usable materials. The **Hot Splash Method** cools slag by spraying water, creating granular slag for cement or construction. The **Hot Stuffy Method** uses controlled cooling in pits, producing dense slag for road bases. The **Roller Method** flattens molten slag with rollers, forming sheet-like products for industrial use. **Other methods** include air cooling or pelletizing, yielding slag for diverse applications like fertilizers or aggregates. Each method tailors slag properties for specific market needs.</p></p>
