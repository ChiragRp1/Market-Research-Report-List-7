Tempered glass, processed using controlled thermal treatments, is stronger and shatter-resistant, commonly used for side and rear windows. Other types include non-safety glass and specialized coatings for aesthetics or UV protection. Together, these market segments cater to vehicles requiring repairs or replacements, ensuring safety and functionality.</p></p>
