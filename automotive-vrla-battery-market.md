<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) Battery market includes AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries use fiberglass mats to absorb electrolyte, offering high power output, faster charging, and vibration resistance, making them ideal for start-stop vehicles and high-performance applications. Gel batteries use a silica-based gel to immobilize the electrolyte, providing deep-cycle capabilities, longer lifespan, and better performance in extreme temperatures. Both are maintenance-free, spill-proof, and widely used in automotive applications, with AGM being more common for modern vehicles and Gel for specialized, deep-cycle needs.</p></p>
