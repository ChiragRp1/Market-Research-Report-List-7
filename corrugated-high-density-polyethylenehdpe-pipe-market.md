<p><ul><li>Single-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipe</li><li>Double Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated high-density polyethylene (HDPE) pipe market comprises two primary types: single-wall and double-wall corrugated HDPE pipes. Single-wall corrugated HDPE pipes are characterized by a single layer of corrugated material, providing lightweight and flexible solutions for drainage and irrigation applications. In contrast, double-wall corrugated HDPE pipes feature an additional inner wall that enhances strength and improves hydraulic performance, making them suitable for more demanding applications like sewer systems and stormwater management. Both types are valued for their durability, corrosion resistance, and ease of installation.</p></p>
