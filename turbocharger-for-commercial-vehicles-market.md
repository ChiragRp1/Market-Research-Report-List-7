<p><p>The **Turbocharger for Commercial Vehicles** market includes **Mechanical Turbocharging** and **Exhaust Turbocharging**. Mechanical Turbocharging uses engine-driven compressors to force more air into the combustion chamber, enhancing power and efficiency. Exhaust Turbocharging leverages exhaust gases to spin a turbine, which drives a compressor to increase air intake, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. Both systems boost engine performance, but exhaust turbocharging is more common in modern commercial vehicles due to its higher efficiency and lower environmental impact.</p></p>
