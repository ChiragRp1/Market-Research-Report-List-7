<p><ul><li>Single Gas</li><li>Dual Gas</li><li>Three Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anaerobic workstation market is categorized based on the gas composition used to create a low-oxygen environment for anaerobic microorganisms. </p><p>- **Single Gas** workstations utilize a single type of inert gas, typically nitrogen, to displace oxygen and maintain an anaerobic atmosphere.  </p><p>- **Dual Gas** systems combine two inert gases, often nitrogen and carbon dioxide, enhancing specific growth conditions for diverse anaerobes.  </p><p>- **Three Gas** workstations incorporate nitrogen, carbon dioxide, and hydrogen, providing optimal conditions for a wider range of anaerobic organisms, particularly in specialized research applications.  </p></p>
