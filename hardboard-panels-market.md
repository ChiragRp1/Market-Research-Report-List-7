Other types include particleboard and HDF (High-Density Fiberboard), each with unique properties like cost-effectiveness or density. These panels cater to diverse applications, from cabinetry to flooring, based on their specific characteristics, such as stability, moisture resistance, or ease of machining.</p></p>
