<p><p>The commercial vehicle steel wheel market can be categorized into three main types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds to create wheels, offering flexibility in design and production. Forging entails shaping steel under high pressure, leading to stronger and lighter wheels. The "others" category includes alternative manufacturing methods such as hybrid processes or advanced technologies. Each method has distinct advantages concerning strength, weight, cost, and manufacturing efficiency, catering to various vehicle requirements.</p></p>
