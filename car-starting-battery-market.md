<p><p>A car starting battery, also known as a starter battery, is a rechargeable battery designed to provide the initial power required to start a vehicle's engine. As environmental regulations tighten, manufacturers are focusing on developing sustainable and recyclable battery solutions, further propelling market growth.</p></p>
